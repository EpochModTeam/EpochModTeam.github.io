        I looked on the Epoch wiki and it sais you can remove buildings? But I have no option in game to do this?

        you can take down building items that you built with a sledgehammer, a chainsaw, or any other tool that causes damage... yes, that includes weapons.

        I think certain items such as the sheds may require an admin to delete, I never tried destroying those.

        With a crowbar (and probably a toolbox) you can remove fresh walls and floors that you just installed, not once you install a door or window though. Be aware, you don't get any supplies from removing building materials. Although you do get something back from removing most crafting items with a crowbar.

        That's my perspective from a players point of view. No scripting involved ;)
