        I've been playing Epoch for a while now and have come up with some items and actions that might make good additions to Epoch. So without wasting time...:

        Industrial Batteries

        Method of acquirement: Industrial loot spawns
        Rarity: Rare
        Description: Large, high-voltage battery that can be used to quietly power lights or, in the absence of generators, to power things such as fuel pumps for a short time. Doing so, however, takes a significant toll on the battery's life.
        Weight: 10lbs
        Actions: Power lightsÂ - Batteries are a quieter alternative to powering lights and with its charge can do so for long amounts of time without and seriousÂ repercussionsÂ on the battery itself. [Gameplay duration: 5 hours of power to lights until battery needs replacing or recharging.]
        Power Fuel Pump - In the absence of generators, batteries can be used to power fuel pumps silently for a very short amount of time. The heavy powerÂ requirement drains the battery completely making it unable to be recharged. [Gameplay duration: 20 seconds of power to fuel pumps and trucks before the battery needs to be replaced. Batteries used for the full 20 seconds cannot be recharged and will not provide power to anything else. Batteries disengaged before 20 seconds must be recharged before being used on anything else.]
        Recharge - Three step process that requires a vehicleÂ and a spool of cable. Using a vehicle, Survivors can recharge their battery for re-use. Their is a possibility of an electrical short which can kill the vehicle's battery [Red Engine].
        Applicable items: Light poles, Landing Lights, Search Lights, Fuel Pumps, Fuel Trucks, All vehicles (for recharge).
