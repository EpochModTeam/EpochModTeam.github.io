        nevermind, I figured it out. Somehow epoch alone is just so gamechanging that the loadtime is severely longer. (and I have macros for Arma) and when doing things on 2 screens it tends to activate in both programs. Due to this it causes a slight overload of shit going on in Arma so it stops responding. Fixed that with some launch perams that quicken load times even with Epoch installed. If you'd like to use this go for it. Load time is approx 30 seconds for me:


        -nobenchmark -nosplash -world=empty

        Hope it helps someone
