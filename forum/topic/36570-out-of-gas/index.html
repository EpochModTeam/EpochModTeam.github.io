        Ran into some bad luck today. Our chopper tipped over for some reason which basically put it completely in the RED. After several Energy Pacs and a repair adapter we got the entire chopper back to all Green. Everyone was excited then... out of gas. At this point everyone remembered " I don't remember ever seeing a gas can/jerry can in Epoch. Ner did we know that our evening had crossed into the twilight zone. Back to goal for the rest of the evening was to find a way to refuel the chopper up on the hill. We never succeeded. We actually bought a refueling truck at a merchant and it spawned above the open air building and literally dropped on our heads. Exploded, killed everyone including the merchant. A lot more misfortune occurred before that final bomb. so we gave up.

        So does anyone know if there is a container that will permit you to refill any vehicle that can't make it to a station and short of having a refueling truck dropped from out of the sky on you heads?

         

        Thanks for Epoch guys.
