        so @juandayz helped me out(thanks alot man) 

        the solution was pretty simple..

        I had 2 "ui_selectslots.sqf" from different scripts, all I had to do was delete/merge one of them and it fixed the whole thing.

        if you have more than 1 script that uses "ui_selectslots.sqf" merge/delete the other(s)"ui_selectslots.sqf"

        thanks again @juandayz
