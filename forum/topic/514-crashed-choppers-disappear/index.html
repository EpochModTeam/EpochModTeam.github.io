        The best way to work out what line to change in the scripts.txt is do do "shift+p" ingame and see what you are kicked for, usually 228 if you have left it unmodified.

         

        Then find where this is "5 "[_this,\"players\"] execVM \"\ca\ui\scripts\dedicatedServerInterface.sqf\";"" and then work out how many lines different to line 228 that actually is. For me thats on line 241.

         

        So to work out the line difference, simple maths of 241-228=13

         

        So find line 126 and add your variance to it and you'll then find what you need :)
