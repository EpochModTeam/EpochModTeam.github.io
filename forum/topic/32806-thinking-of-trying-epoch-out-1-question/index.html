        Dayz... are you talking about dayz epoch for Arma 2? The only way character data carries over is if you are switching between hives servers, which are run by the same group/people. Epoch isnt like dayz standalone or king of the hill where your gear or stats are carried over depending on your GUID or some files on your machine. Everything (that I can think of) is stored on the individual servers database. If there is a set of hived Epoch servers that means those servers simply share certain data from the same database.
