        Just a suggestion for future Epoch mod versions. Rather than having a GUI showing your blood level, hunger, etc, why not just messages that appear for the user, along with sound cues?

         

        For example, once your hunger meter would be down to 25%, why not start having stomach growling and a message saying "You are getting hungry"? Then progressive statements as it gets lower: "You are starting to feel pain from your hunger" etc?

         

        It would help clean up the screen and maybe add a little something to the game.
