        Go to arma2 and disable the expansions. Log out of Arma. Stay logged into steam and start dayz launcher. Add your mods manually there. If only epoch is needed add it only and launch. If overpoch add over watch 1st then epoch then launch. Best way to do it besides ebays. I look at the lists on the launcher. It tells you the mods that need added. There is always a certain order to add them tho so keep trying and you will get the hang of it. (Highlight a server that interests you on the steam side and it will usually show the mods they use. If needed leave Arma load the mods thru the launcher and relaunch. Just make sure you stay logged in steam. Sometimes doubling clicking a server on the launcher will double load your game until you know what your doing a little better.)
