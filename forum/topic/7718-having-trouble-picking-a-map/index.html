        Namalsk is fairly small, and spooky. Good if you like spooky maps.

         

        Panthera is also sorta small, but is very mountainous. Interesting terrain, many people like it.

         

        Taviana is a large map with two islands of course, and small outlaying islands. Parts of the map are glitchy, but its overall pretty good.

         

        Napf is very large, and has the same vegetation feel as Chernarus, but is a lot more mountainous. Overall to me it feels more polished than some of the other maps out there.

         

        Overall I would choose Napf for its size, crannies to hide stuff in, and amount of outlaying islands to build bases on (assuming you play PVE).
