        Your plotpole is assigned to your characterID. When you die, your characterID changes and you no longer "own" it. You need to pull your plot pole up again and replace it or else you won't be able to build within the plot pole's 30 meter range. You'll probably break some toolboxes and crowbars before you successfully remove it though. No one else can place a plot pole within 45 meters of your plot pole unless the server admin has adjusted the range.
