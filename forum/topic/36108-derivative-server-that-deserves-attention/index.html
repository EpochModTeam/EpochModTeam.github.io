        I'm a long time lurker and player, since way back in arma2 days. Epoch Mod is pretty much the only reason I bought Arma 3. I've hopped around on a few Epoch servers, and had the worst experience so far on what is the highest population server for Epoch right now (Epoch Pro.) It appears to be a derivative of EpochMod where they're stripped out any references to [VB]AWOL of the community here, and substituted their own name nearly everywhere, even going so far as to customize the Epoch logo. It seems like a fairly cut-and-dried violation of Epoch server's license.

        Â 

        With this at least one of the highest population "Epoch" servers, I wanted to speak up and make sure you guys here had a chance to try to take the reins back from this server.

        Â 

        As I said they are running a derivative of EpochMod, where all credit to EpochMod, [VB]AWOL, and the team here has been stripped out. They've altered logos (from Epoch to Epoch Pro.) The extent of modifications seems to be mostly limited to a custom vendor system where all vendor prices are jacked, and inventory is infinite. They also have an expanded spawn list, allow you to spawn a dozen or so locations, either on the ground or by halo jump.

        • Website - http://epochpro.clanwebsite.com/
        • Game Tracker Profile - http://www.gametracker.com/server_info/198.27.74.77:2302/

        Their staff is somewhere between suspicious and malicious - they immediately got hostile when I mentioned that they've ripped off Epoch Mod, locking me from moving until they had questioned me. VIPER seems to be the lead admin there, and it's his name that's exclusively credited anywhere through out the mod. They're taking in donations as well (~$100 so far in June, ~$140 in May.)

        They are currently under investigation by the Legal team at Bohemia Interactive.

        If they survive the fallout, best of luck to them, but at this point many here already know to keep them in sight and not to trust them.

        However, customizing the epoch mod with addons and scripts is part of all of this, the vanilla epoch mod by itself is not really popular right now, in fact hasn't been really popular for some time now and what you are seeing is the server owners trying to keep their population happy with new additions and features.
