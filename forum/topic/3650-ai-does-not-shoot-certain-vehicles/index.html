        This addon http://opendayz.net/threads/release-dayzchernarus-mission-system.12169/ works almost perfect besides the c-130 tracks being buggy. The AI is working just fine when you exit any vehicle and approach the missions. I have managed to edit missions to my liking via , difficulty, loot, ect. Currently I am having 2 issues with this addon.

         

        But for some reason the AI do not respond or shoot certain vehicles. For example the camo SUV. My players have just recently let me know of this bug and I am wondering if anyone is having the same issue or can reproduce it? We do not have Sarge AI on the server. 

         

        Also a small bug that goes along with these missions that has been reported, is players not able to enter vehicles with other players, after being involved in a mission. So if player A and B go to a mission together in a Heli, upon completion they cannot both enter the vehicle. The work around is either to log out and back in, or allow the passenger in first then the pilot/driver. 

         

        Does anyone know how to fix these issues or have these issues as well?
