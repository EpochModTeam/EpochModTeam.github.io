        Yes its possible to use the default arma 2 vehicles on your server aswell, here is the difference:

         

        I'll be using the standard LAV25 as an example, but this counts for all of the vehicles.

         

        Regular arma 2 vehicle:

        Classname: LAV25

        - Spawns with Ammo

        - Has Radar

         

        _DZ Vehicles:

        Classname: LAV25_DZ

        - Spawns with Ammo

        - Doesn't have Radar

         

        _DZE Vehicles:

        Classname: LAV25_DZE

        - Spawns without Ammo

        - Doesn't have Radar

         

         

         

        You can pick which vehicle you want based on this selection, and then add the desired classname to the trader.
