        Info on the new patch and gamespy no longer running

         

        Armifer: With gamespy shutdown, Dayz commander is currently using a back up IP list. All Arma2 servers are now through steam, and dayz mod installed through steam, on steam server browser.

        Armifer: Only servers updated to beta patch 1.63.112555 and above will be shown on steam and is not backwards compatible. Dayz Commander will work for now for older servers on 1.62.103718, however epoch 1.0.5 is now released and the hot fix will require latest beta patch, meaning commander will be obsolete for finding your servers

        Armifer: Read here on how to get 1.63.124802 Arma2 version as the 112555 version breaks all dayz servers: http://forums.dayzga...ts-please-read/


        Armifer: Expect over the next couple weeks all servers to migrate to the newer version, which has better performance and reduced bandwidth usage in multiplayer. In the mean time, if you cannot find populated A2 servers, join us in Arma3!
