        Yeah the AI in Sarge can use statically placed weapons. Not sure how you'd get the vehicle to stay stationary though but I can confirm 100% that sarge AI can use the 50's in the UAZ's, and also static placed weapons like the ZU 23 and Kord.

         

        There is a setting in the UPSMON folder somewhere, probably an init or config, that you just need to set to 'true' - it'll be obvious once you see it, it's something like 'Can use static weapons' or something like that. We enabled that and bingo, instant carnage. If you want a statically placed weapon then you really can't go past the ZU 23 for both air and ground defence :D
