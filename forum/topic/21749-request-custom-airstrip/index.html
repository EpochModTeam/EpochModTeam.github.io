        Hi --

         

        I suggest you build your custom airstrip in a nice convenient spot, and then use my "Base Duplicator" tool to relocate your scripts into the ocean -- link in Scripting Resources. One way to build offshore that works very well is to build a pier assembly using the big black concrete blocks, relocate them with a convenient altitude, and then use the editor to build a completely separate airstrip complex and relocate it to the same location with z-values to place everything on top of your piers. Works perfectly. You can build entire cities that way.
