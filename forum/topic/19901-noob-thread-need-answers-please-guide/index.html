        1, 2 : could spawn basicly everywhere

        3: There is the basic multigun and 3 attachements: heal, revive, and repair. You attach them like a suppressor to the multigun. And you need a small energypack to load the gun (10 shots). Then just shoot at the player to heal (on the bodyparts that are hurt), a full heal needs more than one shot. revive= the same, repair: shoot at the parts of the vehicle that are broken.

         

        4 Energy: U need energy for Nightvisiongoogles and for Basebuilding, u get it through driving or sitting in a vehicle, while the engine is running.

         

        5. buildingmaterials are found in any kind of trash, but are very rare.

         

        6,7: Crafting guide: http://epochmod.com/forum/index.php?/topic/17457-crafting-guide-by-rage/?hl=building   and vid about building:
