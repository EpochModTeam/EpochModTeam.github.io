        AI's don't generally shoot at things that out-arm them. Such as tanks and armed helicopters etc. If you equip the AI with rocket launchers then they will fire back. I have noticed that the reaction time of the AI is slower and their aggression is really cut down when the server reaches 50 players or so.

         

        If you wish for the AI to shoot any time of vehicles, I suggest giving them RPGs and Stingers. If you don't like that, try a stationary vehicle, like a stationary rocket launcher with one magazine. Should find hints on how to do it quite easily, both here and on google.
