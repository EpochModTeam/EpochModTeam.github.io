        Hi guys, I just have a couple of questions I'd like answering, I don't feel the need for a large debate on these issues though.

        Firstly, how come there is no toolbox in Arma 3's Epoch mod, meaning we are unable to remove a tyre from a car and place it on another. It takes away from the survival feeling.

        Secondly, why is building disallowed in cities, as this also takes away from the survival feeling. For example, boarding up windows in a small building in a city (cherno etc) as to not draw much attention to yourself. Too add, while I am on about the subject of building, what made you feel to go from a standard plot pole in arma2 Epoch to a HUGE jammer in arma 3 Epoch? As I've mentioned, these take away from the survival feeling of the mod. One last thing, no matter which Epoch server I play on, there are usually lots of people flying around in helicopter and the other half having to walk. There is no balance as in Arma 2 Epoch of having lots of different vehicles. For me, these issues spoil the mod for me, and yea I could go back to playing Arma 2 Epoch, but why would I? Why should I have to go back to substandard graphics and physics?
