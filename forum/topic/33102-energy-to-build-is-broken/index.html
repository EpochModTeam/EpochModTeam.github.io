        Well hello title may be a little misleading ;) 

         

        But i've wanted to bring this up again, I was hoping 0.3.0 would of addressed the badly thought out "Need energy to build" But it has not :(

         

        Ive always wondered what the Devs thought when they added this feature ? And to why they thought it was a good idea ? 

         

        Now see im 50/50 on the whole thing, The idea is great, But the execution is ALL WRONG ! The need for a car,energy pack or standing by a turbine, Just really feels bad !

         

        Why was there never the thought of "Generators" (Remember the things in DayZ-Epoch, That were completely useless, But would of served and amazing part it this)

        But also you should of allowed players to use their stamina as a method of building ! That actually makes more sense, As its more to real life and can be achieved much easier than by lonewolfs ! 

         

        Im only writing this to see other peoples thoughts and maybe the Devs teams thought's on this too 

         

         

        (P.s You guys have done a great job on 0.3.0 , Just allow build base without jammer or allow us to move jammer so it can be put inside base) 

         

        (P.s.s Maybe metal floor panels ?)
