        You should be executing it from a custom compiles not from your init.sqf.

         

        Get your compiles.sqf from your dayz_code\init\compiles.sqf and place that compiles.sqf inside your scripts folder and call it from your init.sqf like this.

         

        Change-

        call compile preprocessFileLineNumbers "\z\addons\dayz_code\init\compiles.sqf";				//Compile regular functions

        To-

        call compile preprocessFileLineNumbers "Scripts\compiles.sqf";				//Compile regular functions

        Then find fnc_usec_selfActions line inside compiles.sqf and change it to

        fnc_usec_selfActions =	compile preprocessFileLineNumbers "Scripts\fn_selfActions.sqf";		//Checks which actions for self

        This should fix your problem. I did not look to compare your code with default code. However you should never call fn_selfActions.sqf like that directly from your init.sqf. Always from a custom compiles.

         

        Edit: After taking a look at your code you have Take clothes by Zabn and EVD pack vehicle script. Everything else looks to be default. I don't see anything that would cause your issue. Do as I said above and your problem should be fixed.

         

        Cheers
