        1. Locked car: inventory will be locked, but gas can be siphoned and parts can be salvaged

        2. If you lose a car key, server admin will need to intervene if you want access to the car again

        3. There's a rare drop hotwire kit off police zombies that has a 75% chance to break into a locked car

        4. Unless the vehicle is destroyed, it will stay there. 

         

        Of course, there are scripts and custom settings that can address the above, but those won't be found on a vanilla server.
