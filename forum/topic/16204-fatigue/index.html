        For anyone that regularly plays ArmA you'd know that the fatigue and sway received a complete overhaul, whether you enjoy the system or not isn't important but what is important is what presence you think that such a system should have in Epoch? I'm asking this as personally speaking, whilst the system is quite in-depth and enjoyable for certain missions it completely ruins others and considering Epoch is unique compared to most other ArmA missions/mods the newly integrated system could prove quite detrimental to the enjoyment factor. I'm referring more to the fatigue than the sway in this instance however I personally have found that the sway regarding some weapons is still quite unfinished and with the broken "Hold breath" on certain weapons (Here's looking at you Lynx) but I digress. The fatigue in vanilla ArmA would almost completely ruin much of the enjoyment factor that would be present in Epoch if every twenty feet you had to go take a bloody nap.

         

        I'm curious to see what other people think of the fatigue and sway in general along with what presence if any they should have in Epoch. I think I've made myself quite clear on the fact that I dislike the intensity of the sway and the fact that with no damage or movement it's still ridiculous on certain weapons and that the fatigue really doesn't have much of a place in anything even close to Epoch.
