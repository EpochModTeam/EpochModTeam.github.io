        I love that you guys have setup a complete endgame experience and there is a multitude of things to do. I did however want to make a couple of suggestions that I feel could be hilarious and fun.
         
        Currently if you are infected and die without treatment on your next spawn you play as a Zombie. I've noticed while playing as a zombie you can run 5 times faster, attack, call horde, and have different vision options. While it is fun playing as a zombie just to screw with people it does get old fast and everyone I've talked to said they just kill themselves if they become one. Some of the causes are while playing as a zombie your temperature falls leading to an infection, which brings on coughing (giving it away that you are a player) and consequently causing you to be infected again. Is there a way to disable the temperature (since there is nothing you can do about it as a zombie) and disable the drinking? I understand the food, that makes since, but the drinking and food are not synced so you tend to run out of drink first. If you don't eat that causes you to lose blood which causes an infection and the cycle repeats. So my question/suggestion is can you remove the ability to get an infection (while a zombie) and maybe make it a small percent chance that attacking another player could cause them to have an infection, which if left untreated would cause them to become a zombie? Then just make temp and drinking disabled?
         
        Also for a completely different experience could you make it instead of becoming a zombie, that you become a bloodsucker? Be able to be invisible and only visible if you attack (like it is currently) then just give the bloodsucker the ability to roar, attack and vision. The roar doesn't have to attract zeds but is instead just used as a scare tactic. The food could go down but the ability to eat zeds and players would make for a lasting experience. I think that would be a fun experience not only playing as one but also fun for the other player as you never know if one is around. Don't have Bloodsuckers randomly throughout the server just have the player be one if they die from an infection, that way the player would always know if they hear one that a player is controlling it.
         
        I think that would be fun!!
         
        Let me know what you think,
        Agent
