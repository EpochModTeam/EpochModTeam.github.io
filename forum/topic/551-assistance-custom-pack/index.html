        Hi Guys,

        As you know I and others have been collaborating some popular scripts and placing them in an updating pack for server owners to employ.

        Whilst this is no easy task alone I/We are also looking for some more assistance.

         

        Slowly the plan is to get a nice team together to run this pack in parallel with the Epoch Mod and also expand to all other maps available with the Mod.

         

        If you feel you could be of assistance in:

         

        Script Creation

        Script Injection/Manipulation

        Mission/Map Editing (Any Maps)

        Graphics and Design (For new Custom Signs coming in soon)

         

        Or even Serer Owners on 3rd Party hosts that are willing to assist with how-to's for your specific providers.

         

        Please let me know here and I can give you TS, Skype and Email contact.

         

        Also membership into the Github now setup for this purpose.

         

        So far it seems to be very popular as it speeds up the process for current server owners and new owners to come.

         

        Thanks to everyone so far for the support and also hope you enjoy the pack.

         

        Cheers,
