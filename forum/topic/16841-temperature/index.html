        Heres what ive been doing.

        As stated above normal is 98.6

        If you are bitten by a snake or rabid dog and get Toxin then your body temp will rise until the toxin slowly goes away. Using a cold pack will lower your body temp by one degree per cold pack.

        At the same time while the toxin is going away then your body will develop immunity.

        As far as a decreased body temp this happens alot at night and while swimming without propper gear. Using a heat pack will raise your temp by one degree.

        I try yo keep my temp above 95 and below 101. But im a bit on the cautous side.

        Hope this helps
