        Arma 3 Epoch is my first Epoch so a post it note would be too much for my knowledge of Epoch history.

         

        I've seen speak on these forums about Missions being implemented.  Would these missions be like they are in Wasteland where they are visible on the map and everyone rushes to them?

         

        Or

         

        Could they just be random spawns of say for example a convoy that randomly spawns that goes from point A to point B along the roads, has AI of course and considerable resources.  Of course the AI should match the value of the resources.  

         

        I like the idea of randomness and stumbling upon it :)  It would encourage exploration.  Imagine being in a town and all the sudden you have 2 transport trucks and 2 hunters full with AI as protection rolling through.  Can you take them with what you have in gear and people at that moment?

         

        I also see the pluses in a broadcast mission with map markers as in Wasteland...it gets players in the same general area which can lead to more action and competition.

         

        Or

         

        Maybe we can have both hehe.
