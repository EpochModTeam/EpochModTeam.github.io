        Guess that as a sandbox mod Epoch allows server owners to provide what their community (or they themselves want).

        That said, the EpochZ: Black Tide and EpochZ: Tanoa servers provide some of what you want:

        - lots of Zombies (mostly in towns and villages) - though no standard ARMA3/Epoch adversaries

        - militarized server, with roaming AI and hard missions

        - Few rules other than no combat in or around safe zones "There are NO safe zones other than the marked safe zones"

        - No virtual garages, base cams or most other things

        We do have Black Market Traders though, and 48-hour vehicle locks (people have lives that can impose). Also some base parts are indestructible (cinder walls, metal floors, HESCOs) but doors and jammers are not.

        If you want to try it out feel free. Plenty of good servers out there to choose from. Experiment and find the one that suits you and your style of play.
