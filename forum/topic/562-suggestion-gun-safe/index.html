        As I always like to have duplicates of weapons,toolbelt items, and even keys; I would like to see a gun safe added.

        As it stands with extra NVGs and storing keys or toolbelt items my safes "fill" up fast.

         

        GunSafe - 50 weapon slots, 20 magazine slots, 5 backpack slots

         

        Should cost the same as regular safe.

         

        that way I can store my toolbelt items and keys in a regular safe and my weapons in another.

        ....In line with this suggestion..

         

        Ever heard of a modular gunsafe? I have one at home. Safe like the one in game, but on the top of it is attached (via bolts inside) is a small secondary safe. Inside of that secondary safe is a locked and loaded pistol along with some important stuff like backup keys and papers.

         

        Point being...

        Add a 'addon key safe', you can then (with a normal safe and addon key safe in inventory) CRAFT a Safe+ or whatever you want to call it.

        It would have 20 extra slots for keys.

         

        Now, knowing the wonderful code in ARMA as i do..

        There's really only weapons/mags/bags soo how to make a safe only able to accept keys might be impossible.

        If so, oh well. This would allow folks to add on to their current safe to fit all the keys.

         

        Between me and my playing partner/roommate, we generally have 3 copies of each key we have (HMMWV M2, Fishing Boat, PBX, RHIB, Kamaz, Etc etc).

         

        Also..a smaller safe would be nice.

        Say  0/20/0 that is like a quarter the size of the current safe. Why? Money safe..

        Id love to be able to have a smaller (and thus harder to find) safe just for my gold.

         

         

        Just a thought.
