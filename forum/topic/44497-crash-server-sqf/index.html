        There are many ways to protect your server.
        First of all if you say that your RCON is taken over, you should definitly edit your RCON and Admin passwords. Only give them to people you really trust. Or use battlemetrics as Rcon tool, then you can whitelist your admins and nobody except you has the rcon password. Also you can setup a lot of rules like kicken vac banned players or auto kick VPN's.

        I've never seen someone who was able to bypass BattleMetrics. And also you can do a lot against script injection. Mostly this is easily done by good battleye filters. There was a topic here on the forum discussing this very heavily.

        I would highly recommend to read everything in THIS topic (to get familiar with battleye and hack prevention):Â https://epochmod.com/forum/topic/43832-hacks/
