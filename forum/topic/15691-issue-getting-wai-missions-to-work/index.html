        Your position on the map marker should be position[]={4254.7,0.00146484, 8542.59}; not position[]={4254.7,8542.59,0.00146484};
         

        The format for those coordinates in this particular regard is (X,Z,Y) not (X,Y,Z).

        Without an error report I wouldn't even know where to begin with your AI issues, I do not use WAI so I am not that familiar with them but I can give it a go if you post where you got the files from, telling me if you have other AI running on the server and post an error report.
