        Posted March 2, 2015

        I had a basic 1x3 base built on 2 foundations.  Maintained my base, next day I'm missing the two floors that my box truck was on and my box truck.  Best guess is that the truck spawned before the floors when server restarted and the truck blew up.  (This was on a PvE server, yes I know, I'm lame, but I wanted to try the new base building without being bothered).  No big deal, I'll fix it. 

         

        The new floors didn't line up at all, when adding a new section, there were gaps.  Ok, if there was an explosion, this is acceptable.  Was trying to move stairs where I was adding a 2nd story to cover the "plot pole" and for some reason I got the floor behind it, not under, behind.  Couldn't get it to reset, kept falling off.  Thought I'd try and take walls off to see if they were causing it to not line up right.  Well in the process of moving the walls, once I had selected it and started to move, I shot back like 20 meters.  Tried to go back up to where I was and shot another 20 meters.  Finally get the walls moved, pick up the floor, it goes flying up with me on it, loses momentum, and falls back to the ground killing me instantly.

         

        I like the idea of Physx for the sole purpose of killing the whole "sky base" thing.  But I think I'd rather deal with skybases than play russian roulette with base building.

         

        TLDR:  Maintaining doesn't work right and base building with Physx is like playing russian roulette.  Never know when you're going to die.
