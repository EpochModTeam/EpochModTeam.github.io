        try to override cfgweap (tell me if u can) 

         

         

        You cannot over-ride cfgWeapons without an additional client addon/mod, cfgWeapons is processed at initialisation of the game and therefore cannot be patched via a mission file.

         

        All of the mission-file patchable cfg* entries are listed on this page on the biki - https://community.bistudio.com/wiki/Description.ext
