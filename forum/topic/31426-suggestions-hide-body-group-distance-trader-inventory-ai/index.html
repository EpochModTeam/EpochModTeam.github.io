        1) Hide/burn body

        Every time we kill people we end up killing them two or three times after that because they run back to try and get their gear. I have to empty out my backpack and move the weapons and gear off the dead bodies. It would be nice to kill someone on the move, hide their body and keep moving without stopping. If we could burn bodies and it required a jerry can, we all agreed we'd carry one around to do it. If we can't sink or burn the bodies then burying or hiding the gear would work.

         

        2) Group member distance/name hotkey
        I play on a wasteland server that if you hold "Left Windows" you can see your group members name and distance right where their hexagon is. It's nice because you don't have to ask who's who and how far they are.

         

        3) Trader inventory Item #
        Instead of scrolling through 600 fireplaces and 200 electronic components I think it would be easier if it just had the item name and the amount that the trader had like "Fireplace 600"


        4) UAV spawned AI IFAKs
        Give the UAV spawned AI Individual First Aid Kits that heal to 100%. Right now we just shoot down the UAV as soon as it spawns, If the AI had FAKs we would fight them for it.

        1. Disagree on hiding body, agree on burning body. Less trolling, higher visability/danger... and make the process lengthy and require a jerry can. I always hated the scroll wheel option in A2 where you could just hide the body... it's not realistic and it's basically a final "screw you" teabagging action upon the player you killed. I think the bodies (if you don't take any action) should remain where they are with the gear on them, just as they died... with the normal despawn timer. Can't hold all of their gear when looting them and have to leave it behind? Well, that's a prioritization issue, not a gameplay issue. If you're worried about them getting their gear back, take their gear and run around and disperse it so they can't find it when/if they run back if that's your perogative. But doing so should include a risk in terms of time/exposure. 

         

        2. I play solo so I can't really say anything on this. 

         

        3. Watching Axles' Twitch stream I asked the same question and he said it's being worked on and should be released soon. I refer to it as "nested trader inventories"... not sure what it'll officially be called. 

         

        4. The UAV mechanic doesn't bother me the way it is right now. They're working on adding more health-regeneration abilities/sources as it is, so I'd wait until they announce that before messing with a mechanic that works pretty well the way it is right now. 

         

        Just my $0.02.

        I really don't understand how hiding a body that you killed is "trolling". The reason myself and my teammates hide bodies or gear of dead enemies is so that they don't run straight back, pick up their weapon and shoot us in the back within a few minutes of them dying. If someone kills you in-game, you should not be able to just run right back and continue where you left off with the gear you had. We end up staying in one area for way too long trying to clean up and hide the gear while fresh spawns are just running right back in. On ARMA2 we would kill someone on the move, hide the body and keep moving like nothing ever happened. That player would come back, see his body was gone, mentally accept his death and start a new life. Either we are playing the game wrong and "trolling" people by not giving them second chance to kill us while they have a new knowledge on our location/weapons/numbers, or I do not understand why getting rid of a dead player's gear is "trolling".
