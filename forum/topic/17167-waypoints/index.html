        I like and dislike waypoints. They make navigation extremely easy. However, in doing so they make the navigationally challenged likely to follow certain routes. MOST of them go to Point A then click on Point B and run in an almost straight line the whole way. Because of that you can easily avoid the high traffic areas or set yourself up to observe them if you wish.
