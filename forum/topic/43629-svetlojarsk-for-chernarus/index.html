        There are different ways to do it but here is the easy way.

        1. Extract your server PBO and make a folder at the root called mapaddons and place the svetlojarsk.sqf file inside of it. Repack your server PBO.

        2. Extract your mission PBO and edit init.sqf

        Find this line in init.sqf 

        execVM "\z\addons\dayz_server\traders\chernarus11.sqf";

        Place this line directly below it. 

        execVM "\z\addons\dayz_server\mapaddons\svetlojarsk.sqf";

        3. Repack your mission PBO.
