        After a player dies or tries to log out of the server, instead of being sent to the lobby they get the Epoch loading screen and get a white screen forcing them to restart the game client and DayZLauncher before being able to join again.

        Host: CitadelServers (I had to update Epoch myself as their version was outdated)
        Scripts installed: DZMS, Epoch Admin Tools, WAI, Service Points, ZSC. (Order in which they were installed, followed all instructions properly)
