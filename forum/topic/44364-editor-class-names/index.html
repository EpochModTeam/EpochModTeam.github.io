        Now i know this may seem as if, at first, is in the wrong subforum, but please let me assure you I feel it needs to be in this section so can be easily found by content creators.

        As most of you are aware, when editing, adding your buildings/vehicles/objects etc, everything comes out good for you. Well almost good. Im writing this post regards to after you put a base addon on a live server, and the fact that, for some creators, wonder why loot is not spawning in buildings etc or pumps are not working when you go to take a drink.

        It all boils down to the class name. So to make things that bit easier Im just creating a list that people can refer to when editing their SQF in order to get things working. I've found a few already from very old posts (of which I had an idea about already) and one or two objects that I found to be the cause of issues ie not working! If anyone finds additional buildings/objects etc they feel should be added to this list just put it in a reply and I will add it to the list.

        Also if admin feel this topic is worthy of being stickied, then please do that, as that will make it easier for members to find and refer to in a glance.
