        So this is my first attempt at a map customization made for our server.

        I decided Balota Airfield is famously a little barren so I extended this to make it more valuable for new spawns but tried to balance the extra reward with extra risk.

        It gets quite zombie heavy and there is limited access.

         

        I'm playing with the idea of making it foot access only (not counting aircraft of course)

        The Barracks area on our server will have a small Static AI group in, we are just undecided on if this should be a Solider camp making it noob friendly or a Bandit camp for more challenge.
