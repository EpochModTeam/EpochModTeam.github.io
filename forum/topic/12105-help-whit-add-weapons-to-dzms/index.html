        I think you're mixing up weapon classnames with weapon descriptions.

        • classnames don't have blanks in them
        • Mk16 and SCAR_L (light) is the same thing
        • Mk17 and SCAR_H (heavy) is the same thing
        • e.g. "Mk16 Mk4CQ/T" is the RL-designation for classname "SCAR_L_STD_Mk4CQT"

        Have a look at http://www.armaholic.com/forums.php?m=posts&q=10843, your list should look like (insert commas, yourself)

         

        "SCAR_L_STD_HOLO"
        "SCAR_L_STD_EGLM_TWS"
        "SCAR_L_CQC"
        "SCAR_L_CQC_Holo"
        "SCAR_L_CQC_EGLM_Holo"
        "SCAR_L_CQC_CCO_SD"
        "SCAR_L_STD_EGLM_RCO"
        "SCAR_L_STD_Mk4CQT"
        "SCAR_L_STD_HOLO"
        "SCAR_H_CQC_CCO_SD"
        "SCAR_H_STD_TWS_SD"
        "SCAR_H_CQC_CCO"
        "SCAR_H_STD_EGLM_Spect"
        "SCAR_H_LNG_Sniper"
        "SCAR_H_LNG_Sniper_SD"
         

        You can add the Vintorez and the M110 like you did but I assumeSCAR_Base is not meant to be a playable weapon.

         

        I guess you'll have to add the required ammunition, 

         

        "30Rnd_556x45_Stanag" //regular

        "30Rnd_556x45_StanagSD" //silenced

         

        for the SCAR_L and

         

        "20Rnd_762x51_B_SCAR" //regular

        "20Rnd_762x51_SB_SCAR" //silenced

         

        for SCAR_H.
