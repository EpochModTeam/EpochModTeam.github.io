        The main conflict with DZAI and Sarge is that each of them sets side relations one way, and the other sets them another way. So depending on the order in which they're loaded, you'll either have DZAI's side relations settings (all sides hostile to player side) or Sarge's settings (east hostile to west, resistance being hostile or friendly). If you take care of this conflict then theoretically you can run DZAI and Sarge together, but I don't know how much Sarge AI micromanages things so there may be other conflicts that aren't obvious.
