        All the information you need... to do everything you want is available with a google search. Matter of fact most of the mods have instructions to follow. If the instructions are too advanced feeling then you would do yourself a favor to learn the basics (again google for the win) and spend a couple weeks tearing apart scripts to understand them. 

         

        If you don't have basic knowledge to follow instructions then we can't really "help" you, we would be doing it "for" you, which won't help you. Do it like we did man, google and read forum posts, read the BIS pages on script commands and what they do and experiment.
