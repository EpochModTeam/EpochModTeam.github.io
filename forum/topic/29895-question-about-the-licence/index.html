        I'm curious about the Creative Commons licence that Epoch is licenced under.

         

        http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-nd/4.0/

         

        According to this it's not against the licence terms to alter the code so long as you don't distribute it, is that right?

         

        • NoDerivatives — If you remix, transform, or build upon the material, you may not distribute the modified material.

        http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-nd/4.0/

         

         

        And as long as it's attributed to it's original owner then the licence terms aren't breached either yeah?

         

        Attribution — You must give appropriate credit, provide a link to the license, andindicate if changes were made. You may do so in any reasonable manner, but not in any way that suggests the licensor endorses you or your use.

         

        So what's the deal with the developers banning people from the forums who simply wish to make changes to the code? I understand if people were to share it back on here but as it is, shouldn't I be able to modify my servers files how I want?

         

        I keep hearing the excuse that they don't want people altering it while it's in alpha due to the extra bugs it creates. Ok, I can understand that but it's not like it's implied that the Epoch dev's should support admins who alter their servers.

         

        There's official servers for vanilla testing, what's the point of official servers if everyone has to run the same as them anyway?

         

        P.S Devs, please don't ban me :P I am just asking because I would really like to be able to change small things on the server and can't understand why it's locked down.
