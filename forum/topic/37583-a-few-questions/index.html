        1. Editing my post. My mistake. Wooden tower is made from wooden floor. Craft that first, click on it and will have option for wooden tower. When you build that, simply hover on the sides of it to add walls with windows which require each 2 lumber packs I think.

        There is only solar energy generator, to build that you have to have 2 electronics components, 2 large metals, a wire, a battery and one or two solar panels. But only wire, battery and solar panels let you even see this option, so you have to find one of them first.

        2. Someone else will answer.

        3. MOVE option was removed in this patch and probably will never come back.
