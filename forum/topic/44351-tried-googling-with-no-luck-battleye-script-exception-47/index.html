@rotoreuter48,

To provide the most help we would need to see the kick from scripts.log and your current script.txt, which varies depending on whether you use the one from epoch for A3 or infistar and how often up update that file.

Disabling battleye is a great way to get a server going but does open the door for those who wish to cause problems. Does anyone remember the A10s raining down all over the sky from the A2 days?

Note that you can temporarily disable all kicks related to scirpts.txt by deleting //new2 from the top of that file.

What I do is disable kicks, add excetptions until I get no kicks, then re-activate kicks.

You can have your server re-load scripts.txt with varied success using Dart or the new RCON from infistar.

There are a few guides on working with battleye filters, including on on epochmod.com:

There is also a nice tool to generate the 'exceptions' for you from eraser1 on exilemod.com

I hope this helps.
