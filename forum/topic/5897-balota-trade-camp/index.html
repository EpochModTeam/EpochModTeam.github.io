        Description (En) (www.translate.ru):

        Hi, everybody. I decided to make the trade camp in Balota. I wanted to make something like base survived. On an entrance to base you will see signs, a lot of garbage and a big grave of the zombie. It is possible to get to camp in two ways: on the ground (you should pass or pass along the strengthened corridor where on entrance you will see a lodge of the watchman, it the managing director of all base) or by air (for this purpose in northwest part of base there is a helipad on 2 seats). In camp there is a parking, a fire, the water crane, toilets, car dealers, food, medicine, the weapon, ammunition, helicopters. As near the seller of the weapon you can test the machine gun acquired by you or the gun, for this purpose the shooting gallery with five automatic targets (three small and two big) there is established.
