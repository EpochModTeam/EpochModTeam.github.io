        OK RUDEBOI, I'm using vilayer as well.... there's a antihack addon on their page, that's fairly good, and also it works with 1.0.2.5.. costs like $20 australian, imo.. worth every cent..

        And you will find that most of the code looks like gibberish to start, but after a couple of weeks, it starts to make sense...

         

        But unfortunately, it is something that we all must learn, for a couple of reason..

         

        And I find the biggest one is, If something screws up, then you have some sort of idea of were to fix it..

         

        Sounds harsh but it's something I've learned as well.

         

        Most of the scripts/addons/mods will work... just follow the instructions, and get used to reading the forums.. both here and opendayz.net..

         

        It will make it some much easier, its not funny..

         

        Just a tip, vilayer have on the addon page on your ACP page... the ability to make it a private hive, and it's easier to add some of the scripts as well, because your modding the files, without having to D/L the pbo's, unpacking and repacking them all the time, and then uploading them again.. its

         

        Biggest tip I can give, organize your pc so that you can BACK-UP anything you mod on your server..

         

        BACK-UP, BACK-UP, BACK-UP always..

        And listen to some of the guru's here as well, they know they're stuff

         

        if you want some help/ get yourself caught, hit me up on here... I'll try to give you a hand
