        I'm just saying that if I want to make like some half-height walls/sandbags (for hiding behind when defending your base on the roof) or create little half-rooms/blocked off areas (for example, protecting your freq jammer from the sides but leaving a slat in the middle to access for maintenance) on the inside of your base. Full wood walls (with current physics) are either unweildy (too big for doing indoor building) or deadly (when they're vibrating/bouncing around). 

         

        For example... size-wise, think back to ARMA 2. The size of the sandbag walls was pretty much perfect for sandbags... and something comparable to the 1/3 wood walls would be perfect for what I'm talking about.
