        I don't know if this is possible... but I'm curious whether or not it is. 

         

        Currently, frequency jammers have a 7-day maintenance period that keeps your base materials from despawning and prevents others from building/moving your base pieces. 

         

        Currently, lockboxes must be accessed and have items moved in/out of them once every 7 days or they will despawn/delete themselves. 

         

        Is it possible to tie the lockboxes to the frequency jammer so that if you maintain the jammer, it essentially "accesses" the lockbox and prevents it from despawning? 

         

        IE: If you maintained your frequency jammer every 6 days for a month and didn't access your lockboxes at all, your lockboxes would remain intact. 

         

        Obviously the current 7-day rule would apply to any lockboxes put down outside of a frequency jammer's range. 

         

        Thoughts?
