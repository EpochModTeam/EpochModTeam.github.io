        So I play as a lone wolf.

        I don't base build yet as I prefer to explore the entire island, scavenge, survive and take on the occasional armed survivor for better gear etc.

         

        My experience so far is that the 3 existing spawn camps, Eastern, Central and Western are simply clustered far too close together.

         

        I would like to see a few more spawn camps spread further apart to make exploring the entirety of Altis better for us lone wolf types who use spawn camps as our return points.

         

        I've put together a small list of possible locations:

        Western:
        no change
         
        Central:
        No change
         
        East:
        Galana Nera Bay
        250,240
         
        South2:
        Mazi Bay
        220,060
         
        South2:
        Naftia Bay
        120,060
         
        North:
        Ammolofi Bay
        100,220
