        Here's a list of stuff I've run into that is bugged with Epoch that I have found.

        Some may be ARMA3-related.

        I will use this template:

        Game mechanic:

        Expected behaviour/outcome:

        Current behaviour/outcome:

        How to reproduce:

        ALL THE BELOW BUGS ARE FOR EPOCH 1.0.3, and currently latest stable (2014-10-05) release of ARMA3, and on ZOMBIES.NU-server

        ---------------------------------------------------

        Game mechanic: Inventory handling

        Expected behaviour/outcome: Changing equipment in the inventory leads to inventory changing places (for example, a gun and mods+ammo gets placed in a backpack, another gun gets equipped with all mods and ammo)

        Current behaviour/outcome: Sometimes things that overlap disappear completely, mods or magazines disappear from the inventory, similar to when a backpack is full in arma2)

        How to reproduce: Low frequency occurence. It seems to occur most frequently when taking out and putting large items in pockets/backpacks, one item gets moved, others simply disappear.

        -----------------------------------------------------

        Game mechanic: Inventory handling

        Expected behaviour/outcome: Equipment in inventory stays between reboots.

        Current behaviour/outcome: Sometimes the complete inventory does not save, we have lost items like half-empty magazines, or equipment that have been moved just prior to logging. Could be related to above report.

        How to reproduce: Low frequency occurence. Managing inventory prior to logging off, possibly just logging off and coming back the next day.

        -----------------------------------------------------

        Game mechanic: Inventory handling

        Expected behaviour/outcome: Moving items between player inventory into vehicle inventory means items move back and forth, including all mods and magazines.

        Current behaviour/outcome: Moving items between player inventory into vehicle inventory sometimes means items just disappear, including weapons, clothing items, etc.

        How to reproduce: Put a car next to some corpses, attempt to move all (or most) of the contents of those corpses into the car inventory.

        -----------------------------------------------------

        Game mechanic: Player trade and inventory handling

        Expected behaviour/outcome: Inventory trading of krypto and items between players means items that are traded get transferred to the player OR are kept because destination is full.

        Current behaviour/outcome: Inventory trading of krypto and items between players result in items disappearing.

        How to reproduce: Trade between two players, move several items, preferrably if player A and B have different equipment and items normally would not fit into destination. Items then will disappear.

        -----------------------------------------------------

        Game mechanic: Trader purchase and inventory handling

        Expected behaviour/outcome: When you buy items like backpacks, or vests, they are applied to the player.

        Current behaviour/outcome: Backpack or vest cannot be purchased because the player cannot fit the vest or the backpack in their inventory.

        How to reproduce: Run as a relative fresh spawn, but with some krypto and try to purchase a backpack or a vest.

        -----------------------------------------------------

        Game mechanic: Trader purchase and inventory handling

        Expected behaviour/outcome: Buy item(s) from a trader, pay the krypto, items are either placed in inventory or secondarily on the ground.

        Current behaviour/outcome: Buy item(s) from a trader, pay the krypto, items disappear from the trader, and from player inventory (because it is full for example)

        How to reproduce: Buy stuff that overlaps player inventory. Sometimes it works, sometimes not, frequency seemingly random? (unknown)

        -----------------------------------------------------

        Game mechanic: Reloading

        Expected behaviour/outcome: Reloading changes the current magazine into the most filled one in the inventory.

        Current behaviour/outcome: Reloading replaces the current magazine with the same one, (eg. the magazine that has 1/30 bullets for example will be placed again in the rifle, instead of the one with 30/30 in inventory)

        How to reproduce: Have several magazines for your rifle, fire 20 rounds, press reload.

        -----------------------------------------------------

        Game mechanic: Ammunition compatibility

        Expected behaviour/outcome: Bullets that are compatible with a certain rifle or pistol can refill magazines that use the same type of ammunition.

        Current behaviour/outcome: If you have a .45 magazine compatible with a certain gun, but not another, those bullets cannot fill an empty magazine for the gun you have.

        How to reproduce: Take a .45 gun and fire 9 rounds of it, have a .45 magazine for another gun, you cannot transfer the bullets from one to the other.

        -----------------------------------------------------

        Game mechanic: Sapper blast

        Expected behaviour/outcome: A sapper that runs up close to a player and explodes, damages or kills the player.

        Current behaviour/outcome: Going prone avoids most or all of the damage from the sapper

        How to reproduce: Go prone next to an approaching sapper

        -----------------------------------------------------

        Game mechanic: Loot spawn

        Expected behaviour/outcome: Loot spawning on shelves of their containers, or above the containers (like trash piles)

        Current behaviour/outcome: Loot spawning inside (invisible) of their containers, or underneath them (so they are invisible)

        How to reproduce: Loot several different containers, and watch the result of their contents.

        -----------------------------------------------------

        Game mechanic: Loot spawn

        Expected behaviour/outcome: Food items spawned in fridges or ovens, weapons/clothing in closets or beds or similar, item placement coherence.

        Current behaviour/outcome: Some items that probably shouldn't be in the oven, is in the oven (for example)

        How to reproduce: Check different loot containers

I also noticed one or two of the other items I picked up were gone from the inventory, and here's what I believe is the reason:

        1. When there is a client-server discrepancy, the client data will take priority UNTIL the server can verify (or not verify) those items
