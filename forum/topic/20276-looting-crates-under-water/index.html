        1. The server you were playing on.

        Skaronator

        2. What arma3 build and what Epoch build you are using.

        Arma 3 1.32 / Epoch 0.1.0.3

        3. What it was you were doing

        Looting crates at shipwrecks

        4. Is it repeatable.

        Yes ( Only tried it at Skaronator, but occured before and after a restart)

        Â 

        Since the last (Arma)update something changed with looting the crates at shipwrecks. As you dont get the inventorymenu under water you could loot the crates with the scrollwheelmenu. But since the last updateof Arma, it seems you can only take one of the 2 items. After you took one of them, the option for the second item disappears. Doesnt happen on the crates above the water, as you can loot with the inventorymenu. It seems the "hand"- symbol appears very shortly but disappears afterwards.
