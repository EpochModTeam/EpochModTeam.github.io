        Well just like to say the latest update, welcomes the return of the Cultist, (I missed this falla).

        However, there are one or two issues I would like to give feedback to.

        The Building System:

        1. Snap building leaves gaps between the wooden walls .
        2. The only way to correct the gap is to remove the panel and loose building materials in the process.  In the past, The  "move" fixed the problem
        3. Please put back the Move option A.S.A.P.
        4. The pressing gear and esc to cancel building mode, didnt place the item back into my inventory. (lost more building gear)

         

        I gave up late last night due to a Boss sapper spawning near the base (jammer), running through  a wall and exploding! thus taking out Me and  two offroads of building materials that took over 14hrs of game play to collect. Two Darters in less than ten minutes whie I am still in the radius of my Jammer.  A fix for the Antagonists being able to walk through walls would be nice.

        The only plus side to the building System I can see,  is the Ladders and Solar panels, Not been able to do any more as a Boss Sapper ended the building experience.
