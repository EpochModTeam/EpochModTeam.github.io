        Did you add them to the startup parameters?

        Should look like the following if you may have to write em by ursfelf.

        Ofc you need to replace the example mods with urs. Usually no ; at the end

         

        -mod=@mod1;@mod2;@mod3 

        -servermod=@mod4;@mod5

         

        Some Hosters also offer drag and drop or something like on and off them in the webinterface
