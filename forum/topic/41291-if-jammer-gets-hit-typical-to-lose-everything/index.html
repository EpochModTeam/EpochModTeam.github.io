        Some like take over base, some prefer to remove it (which is pain in the ass with that "remove item" timer, waste of time in general and very quickly becomes not fun). But yeah, most people if they manage to destroy jammer will take everything apart. So protect it.

        Rant incoming in 3...2....1..

        I have my way of making indestructable base - make floating 1x1 room (everytime you need to maintain base you will have to craft 1 or 2 sets of stairs to reach it) and protect the roof of such room with stud wall or something. BAM. Indestructable base. If any admin/server owner doesn't like it, give him middle finger and say that literally anything in Arma 3 can be glitched through and abused so it is the only way to protect your base against such people. Seen so many servers with retards creating rules "booohoo base must be raidable from ground, booohoo cant protect helis with stud wall, boohoo you must leave your base doors open so that everyone could just take whatever they want!". Screw such people and their thinking. This is mad max world on Altis and unless you play in some kind pussy whitelisted server with 2 people, there will be a time where glitcher comes and simply walks through all your walls and destroys your jammer/safes etc.

        My current base is almost exactly as I described, just that it is aesthetically pleasing and people from outside have no idea that it is in fact floating base :D Even admins clueless as they never play on a server. The only weak spot is my roof as server has build limits too low so I can not protect it good enough but I do not care. I do not want to avoid legit raiders but avoid glitchers and as far as I know, there is no way to glitch through floors (if there is I'm fucked).

        That being said - don't be lazy, you have imagination and everytime you have a design in mind try to think like a bad guy and imagine how would you take over your base. If you can do it, your design is shit. If you can't, you need more imagination and practice :D

        Protip - you can put jammer on the ground horizontally, it does not have to stand vertically, just move it around a little bit and glitch into building structure or just raise it up and let it fall down then pick up. Once it is horizontal you can place it in tiny 1x1 room. Jammer kills if it touches you. Try not to die doing that. ;)
