          Lingor is a great map, the most diverse with multiple airstrips, 3 major airfields and rivers/bays to actually use boats effectively.  As said before Calamar, or any of the 3 large airfields are going to have activity.

         

          The prison is good but over-rated in my opinion since everyone and their mother knows to go there.  You will find military installations all over so just hit those, very few hit the barracks located near the power plants.

         

          Tons of people hide in the woods near the prison, use the NE islands and build on hilltops like Villa Altana.  There's advantages to these but the choke points and the walls surrounding villas can be run down so reinforce asap.

         

          First purchase is a large fishing boat.  Holds 400 items, is cheap and nobody swims out to check them if you park it in way that looks spawned (not up agains land)

         

          My preference is building inside of the large blue-door warehouse/hangar buildings that are Not at airfields to get started.  Pilada is a good area.  Then once you get a small setup you can take the cave by blocking off the entry with cinder and one door.

         

          Once you change over I can log in and show you a few things if you send me your server IP.
