        Must upgrade in order of number. I,II,III,IV

         

        Warning. Vehicle moves and could break legs and cause bleeding during upgrades. 

         

        Performance Upgrade = 2 engine parts.

        Extended Cargo need = camping tent. 

        Armor Upgrade = 1 tank trap, 1 scrap metal. 

        Fuel Economy = 2 Fuel Tank Parts

        URAL CDF (No)

        Gaz Limo (Yes)

        Red Land Cruiser (No)
