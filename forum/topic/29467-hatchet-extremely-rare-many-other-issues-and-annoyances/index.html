        I will list some of the issues/annoyances I've found in an unorganized order. Please do not give me the garbage of "this game is not for u dude xDDD" or "go play cod xDDD" or whatever meme/slogan that can be thrown at me. I list the things I find annoying, take it or leave it (and I don't speak for myself only)--and YES, I KNOW it's an alpha. I know. I know. I know--:

        Â 

        1. After over 12 hours of playing (10+ on an official server) I have only ONCE(!!!!!) found a hatchet and that was from a trader that someone had sold their hatchet to. I have loooted so many things, even TWO CHAINSAWS!!!! - yet never once looted a single hatchet after recent patch.
        This is clearly an issue. Hatchets should be the most abundant item in the game since it's melee range and provides the fundation for playing the game.

        Â 

        2. The flying drone antagonist is extremely "bugged" since sometimes only after THREE seconds of the drone spawning, AI spawn aswell. And everybody knows how insanely accurate, aimbot-like the Arma 3 AI is as opposed to Arma 2 AI.
        I have died SO many times to this annoyingly bugged drone atagonist that I've lost count and smashed my keyboard several times.

        3. Shipping containers contain too little useful loot for the effort it takes to get to one (travel time + danger of enemy encounters + they're rarer than common). All I've found are scrap metal, vehicle repair parts and salvaged metal - none of which are in anyway useful for doing fundamental things such as making a camp fire. It would make sense if shipping containers contained piles of wood, a hatchet, food and drink OR (note: OR) ammunition--whatever else might be shipped in such a large container.

        4. Loot is too random. How does it make sense that a grenade can be found in an oven, or that ammunition can be found in a refrigirator? Ovens and refrigirators need to only contain food items, while beds can contain ammunition, as well as drawers (because these are the places someone would store their weaponry/ammo).

        5. Out of 1000 players, maybe 2 players like "realism"--and mind you, actual realism is impossible to simulate with the current hardware and on such a scale as Arma--so why on Earth force players to be subjected to "physics" when building? Arma 2 Epoch's building system was almost perfect (perfect on the server that had a snapping mod additioned).
        To me it is senseless to take a step back and make the game even harder than it already is.
        Yes, Epoch is hard. It's easy to die, very easy in fact.
        And look at the games that do have "realism" to the mechanics, all of them are TERRIBLE!
        When Rocket (DayZ creator) said he was going to add tons of disease to DayZ standalone, most players were very irritated by it, but due to their childish psychology, they've come to defend what they hate because of the mentally challenged "omg dude dis isn't cod xDDD" meme.
        Isn't it hard enough that we have to traverse a very large map on our bare feet, face the constant chance of dying to enemies, bugs and so on? -- why make the game even more difficult by making building so impossible, and when we can't even spawn at our building (which is fine, I accept it)??? It seems to be a waste of time to begin building because any kid can come over with his 5 16 year old friends and break down a wall and take everything that has been put a lot of effort into securing.

        6. Good weapons such as snipers are too abundant and too easy to get one's hands on. Talk about "this isn't cawla doody dude xDDD" - with the abundance of weapon loot, it DOES actually look like a deathmatch like that game.

        7. Since mobs are client-side, it would make sense to increase the amount of sheep/cattle that spawns nearby, but reduce the nuisance of a dog barking that keeps respawning every 5 minutes and alarming everyone in the vicinity of your presence.
        More zombie spawning would also be lovely.

        8. Would be good if we could at least get the option to get the mod to be more like Arma 2 (by modifying it). I don't see why not, it's not like any of us are going to gain any revenue on it anyway.

        Â 

        9. Thirst should not be quenched so extremely quickly, or at least not later on in the game. Most of the moving in this game is done through running, it's unacceptable having to lie down every 40 seconds for 20 seconds to "get one's breath back".

        10. Would be a great addition if atagonists spawned in military bases much more than otherwise, when a player is present, since most simply barricade the military bases with one or two walls and harvest all the loot they want without any disturbances (and the tower inside the military base makes it very difficult to raid when someone is in there).
