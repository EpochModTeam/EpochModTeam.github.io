        I've recently tried to get my friend to download the mod and to play it with me. Every single server he joins says something with a vac ban, and when I'm in the game it says Playername kicked (vacban #numbers) or something along the lines. Since he is familiar with Arma 3 coding he sent me a couple of lines he said he got from a Epoch config file:

         

        VACBanned = 1

        VACMinimumNumberOfBans = 1
        VACMaximumDaysSinceLastBan = 1
         
        I was wondering if this could be changed since he got banned 5 years or so ago for a Call of duty FOV changer, which later was allowed.

        You could try changing VACMaximumDaysSinceLastBan = 1 to some number lower than how old his VAC ban is. I'm just assuming that would work based on the name of the setting. Or, you could just change VACBanned to 0. These settings are in your server's EpochServer.ini file located in the @EpochHive folder.
