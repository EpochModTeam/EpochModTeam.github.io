        In the UK and probably the US as well, 'Soiled' means you've shit yourself.

         

        So when I see the dubug monitor saying "Soiled 29" I read that as I've shit myself 29 times and I don't think a single moist towelette is going to clean that up!!

         

        I 'think' soiled is supposed to mean your hygiene level, or how 'dirty/smelly' you are.

         

        I would recommend renaming 'soiled to Hygiene to better describe what that parameter is actually measuring.
