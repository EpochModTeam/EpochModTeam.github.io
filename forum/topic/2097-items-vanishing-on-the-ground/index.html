        Am pretty sure this is standard dayz code. To change this would mean going against all of the previous experience of the vanilla dayz team.

        Store your gear in a vehicle, lock box, safe, etc. You can even temporarily add gear to a fire (not many people know that) just don't light it or expect it to be there after a server restart :)

        Anything in the floor will be robbed by passing survivors, Army units, even rabbits have been known to eat gear.

        That's the way it is in this harsh new world.
