        Hello. Just wondering if anyone ever got this to work: http://dayz.st/w/Deployable_Bike/Motorcycle/GyroCopter ?

         

        I've installed it and nothing happens when you have a tool box and scroll wheel or right click the tool box. No option comes up. 

         

        The way it is meant to work is you scroll wheel and click 'Deploy Bike'. You can then upgrade your deployed bicycle to a motor bike if you have 1 scrap metal and 1 engine part. You can then upgrade your motor bike to a gyrocopter if you have another scrap metal and MRA.

         

        Any help is appreciated!
