        In epoch_server_settings.pbo open configs/maps/ and copy one of the mapfiles and rename to malden.h

        Then open it , delete map addons and change at the bottom the trader coordinates to match your tastes in

        staticNpcPos[] = { };

         

        After that dont forget to delete trader pos in the redis db to let them respawn at new positions
