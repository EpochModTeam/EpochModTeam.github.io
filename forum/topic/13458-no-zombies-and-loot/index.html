        where can i find the loot configs?

        On your init.sqf  around line 70  you have those lines:

         

        call compile preprocessFileLineNumbers "\z\addons\dayz_code\init\variables.sqf";

        call compile preprocessFileLineNumbers "\z\addons\dayz_code\init\compiles.sqf";

         

        If you use your old custom  variables and compiles, than you must to update with new one from update.

         

        My zombies and loot start spawning after i use updated those two files.
