        Vehicle & seat: purchased desert camo US Humvee with 50cal M2 protected machine gun

         

        Shooting and killing a player with the gunner seat does not affect the gunner's Murder or Bandits Killed counts. Humanity remains unchanged.

        Confirmed on 3 occasions tonight. Waited for approximately 15minutes, no statistics change. On all 3 occasions, player was not surrounded by zombies, not low on food/water or otherwise in mortal danger.

         

        Have not tested or confirmed with other vehicles. Is this an intentional feature, a bug, isolated incident, or possibly a server misconfig? Anyone else experience similar issues?
