        If you want custom stuff, you're better off (esp. since you're a novice) doing a plain vanilla one first, and move on from there.. I'm an experienced game hoster (hosting myself since 1998) and implementing custom stuff is pretty hard (read trial and error for days).

        But I'm hosting on my own 100/100 fiber lines so don't have to rely on hosting companies either, and can host 2 test servers just because I can.

         

        You also want to ask yourself if you WANT custom stuff : Towing means the map gets cleared out of vehicles by the chopper owners, self bloodbagging decreases teamplay etc etc.

         

        Also : If you're hosting, you get a LOT of abuse coming your way; you're always abusing powers in players' eyes, they always want stuff, and whine if you aren't there.

         

        Find yourself a nice game, and play with your friends, there's MANY that are there forever. ATM after trying plain vanilla, Origins, and now Epoch we landed on Epoch Panthere since that is one of the best maps around, active admins, fast server, just give it a go :)

         

        Look for TZW (you need @panthera as -mod setting, or use Playwith6 to patch)

        Hm, I don't quite remember if this forum has a rule about mentioning commercial services like server hosting, so I won't mention that. Just Use google to find the hosters and compare their services. Every person has their own preferences when it comes to how they want their server to be hosted, anyway.

         

        As for what nedfox mentioned about players whining and wanting stuff from you. there is a simple phrase that I give them: "Admins are law enforcers, not insurance agents or Santa Claus". Make rules for yourself as an admin about what players can ask for or not, and stick to that, no preferential treatment. So if you decide to respawn someone's heli because he disconnected while flying it, you''ll have to do that for every player with the same problem from that point on. Kick or ban players that keep whining because they want preferential treatment so they don't drag down the community.
