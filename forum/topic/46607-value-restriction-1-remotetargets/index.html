        Since you're getting kicked for a "Value Restriction", the filter you need to adjust is in publicvariableval.txt. You are probably being kicked for the leading bracket/parenthesis in your username.

        Remove this entry, or change it to log only (i.e. change "5" to "1"):
        https://github.com/EpochModTeam/DayZ-Epoch/blob/Release_1.0.6.2/Server Files/DZE_Server_Config/BattlEye/publicvariableval.txt#L3
