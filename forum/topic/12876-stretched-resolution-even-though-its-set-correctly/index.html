        Hey, having a slight problem here. My monitor's native resolution is 1280x1024, yet when I set DayZ to that resolution (both Interface and 3D), it seems to be stretched vertically. I'm using a backup monitor until I can afford something better, and I just switched from a HDTV that was a native widescreen of 1360x768. It looked perfect on that monitor, but after changing the resolution for this new monitor, its like its not setting it correctly even though it claims to be.

         

        Anyone else had this before? Know a fix?

         

        Thanks.

        I found the problem. Arma 2 recognized the new resolution, but not the new aspect ratio. You have to delete your config files and then run Arma 2 again for it to recognize a new aspect ratio. Config files are located in Documents/Arma 2 folder.
