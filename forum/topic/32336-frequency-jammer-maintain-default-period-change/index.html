        The current maintenance period is 7 days. The community I play on used a very simple idea to make maintaining the base much easier... they adjusted the DEFAULT maintenance period to 10 days so that you could reliably pick a day of the week and make that "maintenance day" and have a little bit of padding to accommodate "IRL" events that prevent you from getting on and maintaining on-time. Now this is not a troll just to get more time out of the maintenance... it literally just makes things a LOT easier in terms of remembering when the heck you last maintained your base. 

         

        I know, it's stupid cheap to maintain currently but it's just a thought... and doing so on a server community had made things a lot easier in terms of planning maintenance.
