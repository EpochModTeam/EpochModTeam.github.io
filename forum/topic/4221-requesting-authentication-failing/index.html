        Sounds like you need to re-install battleye on your pc.  Also, does this only happen with one server or all of them? If its all of them, then try re-installing Arma from scratch, make sure that works afterwards first, then add the mods again.  Use Dayz Commander for the mods.

         

        From experience, uninstall arma and all your mods, reboot your pc, then install arma , reboot , install dayz commander and install your epoch mod and you should be good to go.  Also, and I'm not pointing the finger, just saying, that the error you're getting can also be caused by not properly un-installing hacking tools, or running hacks that aren't up to date (even running bypassers to get around battleyes guid checking etc).
