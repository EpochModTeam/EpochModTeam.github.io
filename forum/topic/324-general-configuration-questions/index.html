        Custom Scrips/Addons I can answer to a degree:

         

        I like where Epoch is going and give the Development team my full support, however I also enjoy some little extras that aren't available either just yet or never at all.

        In saying that Epoch as its open development gives you access to all the files needed to customize your own experience if you so choose be it by client or server side addins.

         

        I am working on a server side adding for owners to employ and choose from my selection what they want to add.

         

        More information can be found here: http://dayzepoch.com/forum/index.php?/topic/334-pre-release-custom-server-pack/

         

        I will do my best to update this in parallel to any changes/new features that Epoch adds when patches are released and provide any feedback support to server owners choosing to employ them.

         

        Vehicle spawns

         

        In my opinion are how they should be.. too much was it abused that server owners would spawn specific vehicles for themselves to play with. No more!

        As a server owner I do slightly miss this for bug compensation and other things but with money you can get vehicles with keys if your an admin anyway with gold.. 

         

        As for the Air vehicles kicking around, you could start a fresh with the vehicles completely restricted/removed from your DB and vehicle lists.

        Also add BE Kicks for entering the class.

         

        Max vehicles should increase the spawned overall after server restart I am sure, but as there are always vehicles everywhere I am yet to confirm the actual number on the map at one time.

        Don't want too many otherwise or everyone will just be selling them and getting stinking rich!  :D
