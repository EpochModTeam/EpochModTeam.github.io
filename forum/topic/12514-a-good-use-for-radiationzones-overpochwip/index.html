        I thought up a good use for radiation when building in a remote location. I wanted an option to kind of "funnel" potential enemies a specific way if they were to approach my base. I thought, if there were lot pole type objects with radiation warnings that would emit radiation (with the effects you describe) within a certain radius then we could deter looters and/or make them choose a route to our base that we determine.

        Essentially a customizable moat of radiation.

        So I love your idea!
