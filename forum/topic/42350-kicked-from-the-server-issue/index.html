I've spent the past 12 hours trying to fix this issue, and i've done almost everything. However, i found out something really interesting for this issue on my specific case of this.

After sitting here all day trying to fix this, I asked my SO if i could play on her PC. She said yes, so I got on her PC and logged into my Steam account. I got the "Kicked from the server" issue again. So, I logged out and logged into HER steam account, and all of the sudden, it worked like a charm! I believe it has something to do with my STEAM account, which makes no sense. I've never been banned from a server, as I only have had the game for 2 days and all of the time has been spent trying to fix this stupid issue.

Yes, I've reinstalled, twice.

Yes, I've port forwarded.

Yes, I've turned my firewall off and uninstalled all of my antivirus.

Yes, I've restarted my PC after doing all of these things.

Yes, I've tried deleting my add ons folder and then verifying cache.

Yes, I've tried verifying my cache, only to have no files corrupted/missing.

I'm probably forgetting some stuff. I've been at this literally all day long. The only thing I haven't done is ask for help, because I understand that this is a very common issue.

If anyone knows anything that could potentially help me, please let me know. I can play on ONE specific server, but no other ones.

Again, when I had switched to my girlfriends PC, while remaining on my steam account, it did not work. But when I switched steam accounts over to hers, it miraculously worked.
