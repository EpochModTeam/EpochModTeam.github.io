        There are very few good small maps that 'work' with the Dayz Epoch mod. Most of them are built for the purpose of battle simulations. So there are very few enterable buildings or big towns or cities. Most of them are desert or large open land with an airfield in the middle. The best maps for Dayz/Epoch have already been used and are the ones still in the Dayz launcher list tbh.

        My personal favourite smaller map is Thirsk & the winter variant. You can find the maps on Armaholic Thirsk -Map

        Jade Groove is on Armaholic and it has server keys too if thats what you're looking for -Â J.GrooveÂ 

        Takistan, although not one of my top 5 maps, has always been good for server performance in my experience.
