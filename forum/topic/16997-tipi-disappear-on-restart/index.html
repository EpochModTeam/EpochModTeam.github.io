        Server: OCAU#01

        Time: ~1015 AEST (+9.30) restart.

        Bug: Duping, and TiPi despawn on restart.

         

        A group of us had a TiPi build into rocks up the northern coast. On restart it had despawned.

         

        Events leading up to the despawn.

         

        - 2 members of group loot a dead body.

        - Both try to take his clothes, and they get duped.

        - Both members are wearing the same outfit, and the body still has them on him.

        - 1hr+ of normal play.

        - Back at base, Player 1 crafts foundations.

        - Player 1 tests biulding (does not place) foundations.

        - Player 2 is 100s of meters away, and now has foundations in inventory.

        - Player 1 does not.

        - Player 2 can now test/place foundations.

        - Player 1 cannot walk forward by pressing 'W.' It now makes him walk back slowly.

        - Player 1 gets stuck in a rock, and dies.

        - Player 1 is stuck on the reviveable countdown timer.

        - Server restarts.

        - Player 1 is stuck on countdown timer in debug.

        - Player 2 does not have foundations in inventory.

        - TiPi, and all contained loot has despawned.

         

        We are unsure if the duping is related to the TiPi disappearing, but would think it would be responsible for what happened to the foundations.

         

        Overall it was quite weird, but hey, it is an alpha.

         

        Just annoyed we lost the hatchet. Those things are rare!
