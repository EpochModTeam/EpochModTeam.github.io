        If you're talking about adding custom content then you can look through the threads in this forum to find custom .sqf files, usually accompanied by screenshots to show what it will do. Most explain how to add them, its as simple as adding the files to a folder either in your server-side files or in your mission file, then by calling them. As I said there is more info on this on other threads but that is how you can add custom content to your server. Safezones depend on what code you are using, there are many systems out there. My servers currently use infistars version, however a little looking and you should be able to find some installation guides.
