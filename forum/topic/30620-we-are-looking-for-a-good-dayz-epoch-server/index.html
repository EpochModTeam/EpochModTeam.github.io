        we, me and my m8s from the funclan [TR] (about 7 peoples), are looking for a new good DayZ Epoch or Overpoch server. Our requirements are the following:

         

        - german or/and english language

        - no crosshair

        - no TWS

        - no tanks and vehicles like that (arm. SUV / HMMWV GPK / arm. Vodnik / arm. MI / UH-1Y and similar vehicles are ok)

        - no RPG and weapeons like that

        - indestructable cinderblock bases

        - missions should min. be middle oder hard to do and the loot of them not to much (for example no 7 cinderblocks or cinderblock walls for a lower mission)

        - chernarus (max. 30 players) or napf (max. 40-45 players)

        - plotpole for life with maintain every 14 days or more

        - should not be to easy to get stuff but also not very hard (normal loot I think)

        - prices for vehicles, weapons and other stuff should not be to low

         

        Someone know a server that could be convenient? We are open for all and would test all suggestions :)

         

        Thank you for your help :)
